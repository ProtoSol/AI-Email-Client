About AI Email Client

Welcome to AI Email Client - a modern email management solution powered by artificial intelligence. Our platform combines traditional email functionality with cutting-edge AI features to enhance your email experience.

Core Features

AI Summarization

Get instant summaries of long emails using advanced NLP models, saving you time and improving productivity.

Smart Categorization

Emails are automatically organized into categories (General, Work, Personal, Important) for better management.

Email Analytics

Track your email patterns with detailed statistics and insights about your communication habits.

Key Features

  • Modern, responsive interface with smooth animations
  • Advanced search functionality for quick email retrieval
  • Secure authentication with password reset capability

    Technology Stack

    Backend
    • Python 3.10
    • Flask Web Framework
    • SQLAlchemy ORM
    • Flask-Login & Flask-Bcrypt
    Frontend
    • Bootstrap 4.5
    • HTML5 & CSS3
    • JavaScript

    AI Integration

    Our application leverages the power of the Transformers library by Hugging Face, utilizing BERT-based models for:

    • Email summarization
    • Smart categorization
    • Content analysis

    Getting Started

    To begin using AI Email Client:

    1. Register for a new account or log in if you already have one
    2. Customize your profile settings
    3. Start composing and receiving emails
    4. Use the "Summarize" button to get AI-generated summaries
    5. Explore your email statistics in the sidebar
    Note: For security, passwords must be at least 8 characters long and contain a mix of letters and numbers.
